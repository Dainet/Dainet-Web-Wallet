More About Dainet




The purpose of the Dainet token

Dainet token is used as the main source of currency to interact with the Dainet network, to handle transactions and settlements within a distributed network of artificial intelligence nodes.

ERC-20 standard

Tokens are hosted on Ethereum blockchain. Therefore, the address of the Dainet Wallet is also an Ethereum address. You can send Dainet tokens to any Ethereum address and have access to it through MyEtherWallet.com. Read more...
